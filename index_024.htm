Publicity Coordinator
The publicity coordinator oversees the communications committee to make our organization and projects known in the community. The goal is to attract all walks of life to WinnipegCORE in all our Winnipeg communities inspiring students, seniors, schools, organizations, businesses to keep this dream alive and growing.
Specific duties include:
- Renewing current advertising and placing new advertisements as required in various sites such as
   local newspapers and community events listings online.
- Maintaining the WinnipegCORE Facebook site with updates regarding school events and any
  relevant topics of interests.
- Placing advertisements in publications in both english and french languages.
- Preparing advertisements for posting on public bulletin boards at community centres and libraries
- Working with the vice-president and partners to ensure successful open houses (Educational Tours)
at the Orchard, updating and advertising the open house signage.
Qualities:
- Effective communication skills
- Power-Point Presentation skills
- Public Speaking
- Good eye for PR opportunities
- Speak and write French (prefered)
