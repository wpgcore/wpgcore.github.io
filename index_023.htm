Orchard Maintenance Coordinator
The Orchard maintenance coordinator works directly with on-site volunteers/workers to ensure that the orchards are properly maintained, cleaned, pruned, and in tip-top condition. This role requires the individual to periodicaly check in with the onsite orchards manager in care of orchard; making sure the orchard is in the best fruit performing state.
Specific duties include:
- Observation of Orchards use and care
- Suggestions over improvements, repairs, and re-planting
- Providing a report on each orchard at start and end of season
Qualities:
- Nursery background & Strong background in fruit trees is a must
- Must know grafting techniques, pruning, soil conditions, fruit tree varieites, plant growth
   requirements, etc...
- Able to communicate requirements and expectations to onsite Orchard staff, schools and volunteers
 
- Educate and pass down best practices to individual(s)
- Participating in board meetings
