Demonstration Orchard Class
This Orchard is designed with benches, arbours and lattice to support vines, surrounded by edible shrub species on the outside parimeter with a classic espelier fence.
 
The concept of the orchard is to call it the main home orchard showing everyone that in our inner city we are focused on the future of food security.
 
Educational tours of the Orchard will begin 2013-2014
