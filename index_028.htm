Youth Ambassador Coordinator
The Youth Ambassador Coordinator oversees the Educational area of WinnipegCORE.
The Youth Ambassador Coordinator is in charge of building and running a youth committee of students that represents their own school. You will through education assist as a committee to help participating students establish an orchard for their own school, with the continous help of WinnipegCORE in the background empowering youth to build, create, and grow local food.
Specific duties include:
- Liaising with various schools,organizations offering fundraising opportunities
- Educate schools that our program empowers all youth to participate and grow with community
- Promoting WinnipegCORE through its mission and core values
- Directly working with all schools that will register in our program
- Working together with our board of directors, making sure each school succeeds
  building a sucessful Community Orchard.
- Continue each year monitoring orchard success through a Youth Ambassador Committe
- Work together with fundraising coordinator
- Working with secretary to issue communications regarding fundraising events and activities
- Working with the publicity coordinator on progress new projects and successes to public

Qualities:
- Strong background on fruit trees, nursery operations
- Good PR skills
- Effective communication skills
- Background in Education
- Loves to learn and teach youth
- Ability to commit 10 – 15 + hours a month
- Well Organized
- Implementation skills
