Community Orchards For Schools
Mission Of The Program
Is to equip all students with the knowledge and skills to help them graduate from their school inspiring them to discover their own sense of purpose, empowering them to become quality ambassadors for their community contributing to sustainable ways of living and acting in our city, and the world.
What it takes
*Community that functions on trust
*Communication and risk taking
*Rigourous academic curriculum and interdisiplinary approach everything is connected
*Partnerships and synergies
Change starts here...with you!
Imagine what kind of future is possible when we are all part of an empowered and connected community.
