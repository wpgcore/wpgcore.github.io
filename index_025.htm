Fundraising Coordinator
The fundraising coordinator oversees the fundraising committee in its function to
organize fundraising events and activities throughout the season/year.
Specific duties include:
- Liaising with various organizations offering fundraising opportunities
- Tracking fundraising contributions from each organization
- Working with secretary to issue communications regarding fundraising events and activities
- Planning the bi-annual raffles
- Reporting on the profitability on each fundraising event
- Working and communicating directly with the partnerships
Qualities:
- Effective communication skills
- Ability to commit 10 – 15 hours a month
- Organized
- Energetic spirit required promoting free locally grown fruit/food
- Loves to get involved with community and know how to promote WinnipegCORE's values/mission
