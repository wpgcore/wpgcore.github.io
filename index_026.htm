Volunteer Coordinator
The volunteer coordinator is responsible for coordinating all volunteers for project(s), with the exception of the Board of Directors.
Specific duties include:
- Receiving volunteers’ volunteer sign-up forms
- Assigning volunteers to volunteer positions
- Informing all volunteers of their jobs & job descriptions
- Tracking participation in volunteer activities
- Working with community, schools, organizations, partnerships to ensure that all volunteers work
   together in a community fashion
- Reminding clean-up teams of their upcoming duties
Qualities:
- Excellent communication skills
- Assertive
- Organized
