The Community Urban Orchard Program
Building Communities With Community
Mission Of The Program
Is to equip all citizens of our community with the knowledge and skills to grow, harvest and maintain their community orchard, inspiring them and help in discovering their own sense of purpose, empowering them to further educate others in their community becoming quality sustainable stewards of our city, and our world...
What it takes
*Community that functions on trust
*Communication and risk taking
*Working together as a community participating growing, maintenaing & harvesting
*Partnerships and synergies
Change starts here...with you!
Imagine what kind of future is possible when we are all part of an empowered and connected community.
