Secretary
(Volunteer)
SecretaryThe secretary maintains the records, and is responsible for communications between the board and WinnipegCORE's partnerships.
Specific duties include:
- Preparing and distributing agendas for board meetings
- Recording and distributing minutes of meetings
- Updating records of bylaws and policies
- Assuming responsibilities of Privacy Officer
- Working with the webmaster to ensure that up-to-date information is posted on the website
- Informing the webmaster in a timely fashion of changes to the Board mailing list
- Maintaining the Board Calendar
- Preparing and distributing any newsletters and communications the board may wish to issue or
  distribute
- Preparing, or assisting the president with preparation of Powerpoint presentations for the General
  Meetings.
Qualities:
- Effective written communication skills
- Able to attend all meetings
- (Optional) Able to be the go-to person for information on policies, political help, other organization
  potential partnerships
- Record keeping
