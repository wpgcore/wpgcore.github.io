If you are a school, or organization who is looking for help building a community orchard within the city limits, you may apply for an orchard on the left by filling out the application form in detail.
If you are a resident of Winnipeg that would like to have a community orchard available to you in your area, you may also fill out an application form provided on the left, so we can identify your area by compiling enough applications required for WinnipegCORE to help and assist you in building a community orchard in your neighbourhood.
